WikiWho? Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

WikiWho? collects the minimum data needed to provide the game experience:

  • Account data: Email address (if you create an account), username, and avatar selection.
  • Game data: Scores, game history, achievements, and challenge results.
  • Social data: Friend connections and friend codes.

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or any data unrelated to the game.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Save your game progress and stats across devices
  • Display leaderboards and achievement badges
  • Enable friend connections and challenges
  • Improve the game experience

Third-Party Services

WikiWho? uses Supabase for authentication, database, and file storage. Your data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure. Please refer to Supabase's privacy policy for details on their data handling practices.

If you sign in with Google or Apple, those providers handle the authentication flow. We only receive your email address and display name from these providers.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data (email, username) is removed. Anonymized game scores may be retained for leaderboard purposes.

Children's Privacy

WikiWho? is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us, or directly from the app (Profile → Delete Account).

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
